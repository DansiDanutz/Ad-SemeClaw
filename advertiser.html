Your project, in front of engineers — exactly when they're paying attention.

ad-NOW is the advertising layer for SemeClaw. Every time a developer or AI team spins up a meeting, they watch a short Sponsored Spotlight while their agents assemble. That's your slot.

Meet SemeClaw — the war room where your product ideas get built.

SemeClaw is an agent orchestration platform. You launch a pipeline, a crew of AI agents (Research · Architect · Strategist · Coder · Writer · Paperclip) assembles in a meeting room, and your project gets planned, coded, and shipped — live, while you watch.

Every meeting starts with a short prep window while the agents connect to the droplet fleet. That prep window is the slot ad-NOW fills. Developers who are actively shipping see your card at the exact moment they're open to discovering new tools.
